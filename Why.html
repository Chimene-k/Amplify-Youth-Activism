Step 2: Reflect and Absorb

Here, we will reflect on what we have learned so far and figure out how to apply what we learn to our activism. We will navigate the process in three stages: Internal Reflection, Absorb, and Apply to Action.

Internal Reflection

This stage is going to be all about asking yourself questions and internalizing the lessons and information you have learned. This process will likely involve introspection: the examination or observation of one's own mental and emotional processes. This part of the process is completely reliant on you.
